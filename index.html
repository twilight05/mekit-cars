01 — Overview

Problem Statement

One vehicle business still relies on scattered channels — phone calls, social posts, and word-of-mouth — to advertise what it offers. There is no simple online catalog where people can see what’s available (a limo, a sedan, an SUV, etc.) and request it without calling first.

This product is a car listing platform for that single company: the business publishes its vehicles with photos and descriptions; visitors browse the catalog, tap a listing they want, read the full product page, fill out a short inquiry form, and that submission is delivered directly to the owner’s WhatsApp. Not a multi-vendor marketplace — one company, one WhatsApp inbox for all leads.

How It Works

Example: someone needs a limo. They open the site, see the limo in the grid, tap the image, land on the product description, fill out the form, and the owner gets the request on WhatsApp — no phone tag, no lost DMs.

1. Browse: Visitor sees available vehicles (limos, cars, etc.) on a listing page — each shown as a card with a photo, title, and key details.
2. Tap & view: Visitor taps the image (or card) and opens the product description page — photos, specs, price, and full description for that vehicle.
3. Inquire: On that page, visitor fills out an inquiry form (name, phone, dates/message, etc.).
4. WhatsApp delivery: On submit, the form data is sent straight to the business owner’s WhatsApp so they can reply and close the booking.
Admin (behind the scenes): Company staff log in to add, edit, and remove listings and keep inventory up to date.
02 — Users

Who Is This For

Visitors (public)

People who need a vehicle from this company — e.g. hiring a limo for an event. They browse listings, tap one they like, read the product page, submit the inquiry form, and wait for a WhatsApp reply. No account required.

Company staff (admin)

Employees or owners of the single business behind the platform. They log in to add, edit, and remove listings, upload photos, and mark cars as sold. All inventory belongs to this one company.

03 — Features

Feature Scope

Core capabilities for a single-company car listing site. Must-have items ship in the 1-week launch; nice-to-haves follow immediately after if time allows.

Must have
Inventory listing page

Grid of available vehicles (limos, cars, etc.). Each card shows a tappable photo, title, and key info. Tapping the image opens the product description page.

Must have
Search & filters

Filter by make, body type (sedan, SUV, truck…), price range, and year. Text search by make/model. Results update in real time.

Must have
Product description page

Opened when a visitor taps a listing image. Full details: photo gallery, specs, price, and description — plus the inquiry form below.

Must have
Inquiry form → WhatsApp

Short form on the product page (e.g. name, phone, date, message). On submit, the inquiry is formatted and sent directly to the owner’s WhatsApp number.

Must have
Admin panel — listings

Add / edit / delete listings, upload photos, set price and specs, mark sold/available, and configure the WhatsApp number that receives inquiries.

Must have
Login

Secure sign-in for company staff to access the admin dashboard and manage the business’s car listings.

Must have
Mobile-first responsive design

Full experience on mobile browsers. Cards, filters, and gallery all optimised for touch. Fast load times on slower connections.

Nice to have
Sold badge / filter

Mark cars as sold with a visual badge. Option to hide or show sold cars. Builds trust and shows throughput.

Nice to have
Share listing

Native share button on each listing page so visitors can send a car link via WhatsApp. Drives organic referral traffic.

Nice to have
Favourites (local)

Save cars to a shortlist stored in browser local storage. Resets on clearing browser data.

05 — Pages & Screens

Core Pages

Homepage / Listings page

Grid of everything the company offers. Each item is a card with a tappable image — tap opens the product description. Optional filters (type, price, etc.). Example: visitor scrolling sees a limo, taps the photo.

Product description page

Single-vehicle page after a tap. Swipeable photos, specs, price, and written description. Below that, the inquiry form — visitor fills it in and submits; the owner receives it on WhatsApp with the vehicle name included so they know which listing the lead is for.

Footer / business info (optional)

Company name, location, or hours in the site footer. General enquiries still go through listing forms or the main WhatsApp number configured in admin.

Login page

Sign-in for company staff only. Redirects to the admin dashboard after successful authentication.

Admin dashboard

Internal tool for the one company running the site. Manage listings (add, edit, delete, photos, sold/available). Set the owner WhatsApp number that receives all inquiry form submissions.
