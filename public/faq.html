FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about browsing, inquiring, and booking through our platform.

How do I rent a vehicle?

Browse our showroom, open any vehicle, and tap Rent Now. WhatsApp opens with full vehicle details ready to send.

What happens when I submit a form?

Your inquiry is saved in our system and a pre-filled WhatsApp message opens on your device. Tap send in WhatsApp and our team receives it instantly with all your details and the vehicle information.

Can I book without WhatsApp?

WhatsApp is our primary channel for fast responses. You can also call or email us using the details on our Contact page. For the quickest reply, we recommend WhatsApp.

Are prices per day or per event?

Listed prices are starting rates. Final pricing depends on duration, distance, and event type. Share your dates in the inquiry form and we'll confirm the exact rate on WhatsApp.

Do you provide chauffeurs?

Many of our vehicles are available with professional chauffeur service. Mention your preference in the inquiry notes and we'll confirm availability and pricing.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Lagos and surrounding areas, with nationwide availability for select events. Contact us with your location and we'll confirm coverage.

How do I save vehicles to compare later?

Tap the heart icon on any vehicle card to save it. Return to the fleet page and enable "Saved only" to view your shortlisted vehicles.
